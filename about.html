Our Story

HD Heed was founded by a team of dedicated Registered Nurses who bring together more than 15 years of combined experience in disability and aged care. Having worked closely with people of all abilities, ages, and backgrounds, our team has seen firsthand the importance of person-centred care that is not just clinically sound—but warm, respectful, and empowering.

From supporting individuals with high physical needs to guiding families through dementia care and daily living challenges, we understand what quality support truly means. At HD Heed, we believe care is not just a service—it's a relationship, a promise, and a commitment to dignity and independence.

That’s why we created HD Heed: to bring heart and excellence together in every interaction. Our goal is to provide consistent, compassionate care tailored to each individual’s goals and lifestyle, while giving families peace of mind. Whether it’s a few hours of in-home help or ongoing support, we aim to make every moment meaningful.
