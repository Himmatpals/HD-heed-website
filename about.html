Meet Your Care Team

Himmatpal Syan

When you choose to work with me, you can count on over five years of hands-on expertise in the disability and aged-care sector delivered with unwavering commitment and responsiveness. I understand that every person’s journey is unique, so I’ll partner with you to co-create a support plan that not only meets regulatory standards but also reflects your goals, preferences and lifestyle. Whether it’s a prompt wound-care treatment, a tailored behaviour-support strategy, or seamless coordination with allied health professionals, I’m dedicated to providing high-quality care exactly when you need it. You’ll always hear from me in clear, respectful language—no jargon—so you know what’s happening, why we’re doing it, and when. My promise is simple: compassionate, person-centred support delivered on time, every time, so you can focus on living with dignity and confidence.

Jagdeep Kaur

I’ve spent the last five years immersing myself in the disability and aged-care sector, and what I’ve learned most is that genuine connection comes before anything else. I draw on my background in behaviour support, neuro-observations and medication coordination to build care plans that fit each person’s daily rhythm—whether that’s perfecting a wound-care routine or finding the right community activity to boost confidence. I love rolling up my sleeves alongside families and allied-health teams, brainstorming creative solutions—from modifying a bathroom for independence to choreographing a small garden outing—that spark real moments of joy. Friends and colleagues appreciate my patient approach: I listen first, ask the tough questions, then adapt on the spot when needs shift. At the heart of my work is the belief that everyone deserves both expertise and empathy, and that together we can turn care into opportunity.
