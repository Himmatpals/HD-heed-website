Daily Living Skills Support

What is it?
Daily living skills support (sometimes called life skills training or capacity building) is a service that helps you learn and improve the skills you need for everyday independence. Instead of doing things for you, our support workers or mentors will work with you to practice and build confidence in a variety of tasks and routines. The ultimate aim is to empower you to do more on your own, making day-to-day life easier and more fulfilling.

What Skills Are Included?

It can cover an array of areas – essentially anything that will help you live more autonomously. Common focuses include:

  • Personal care skills – showering, grooming, hygiene
  • Home care – cleaning, laundry, dishes, basic cooking
  • Meal preparation – planning, shopping, cooking
  • Money management – budgeting, banking, paying bills
  • Social & communication – phone/email, relationships
  • Travel training – public transport, crossing roads
  • Time management – routines, calendars, reminders

How the Training is Delivered

Life skills support can happen one-on-one in your home or community, or in a small group/workshop setting. Activities include real-life practice such as shopping, meal prep, and budgeting. Our team takes a patient and adaptive approach, tailoring each session to your pace, using visual tools, and celebrating achievements.

Benefits of Life Skills Development

This support increases independence, builds self-esteem, boosts safety, and encourages participation in the community. It lightens the load for family carers and prepares participants for more independent living. Even small progress brings greater autonomy and confidence.

Examples of Daily Living Skills Support

  • Morning routine assistance – developing healthy habits
  • Weekly cooking practice – learning kitchen safety & skills
  • Budgeting and shopping training
  • Social skills development through community outings
  • Public transport use with guided practice

Tailored to Your Goals

Our support adapts to your needs. Whether it's preparing for employment, social engagement, or just doing things more confidently at home, we help you achieve it step-by-step with compassion and encouragement.
