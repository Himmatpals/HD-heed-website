Nursing Care

Personalized healthcare and support delivered to your home.

Comprehensive Nursing Support

At HD Heed, we offer professional nursing services to support individuals with high care needs, enabling them to live independently at home. Our registered nurses deliver a range of services tailored to medical and personal health needs, ensuring comfort and safety in the familiar environment of your home.

What Does Nursing Care Include?

  • Medication Management: Administering medications, ensuring proper dosage, and timing. This includes managing injections, oral medications, and overseeing chronic condition treatments.
  • Wound Care: Cleaning, dressing, and managing wounds or pressure sores to promote healing and prevent infections.
  • Respiratory Support: Assisting with ventilators, oxygen therapy, nebulizers, and other respiratory equipment.
  • Catheter and Continence Support: Help with managing catheters and ensuring proper hygiene.
  • Diabetes Management: Monitoring blood sugar levels, administering insulin, and providing guidance on diabetes management.
  • PEG Feeding: Assisting with tube feeding (if required), ensuring proper nutritional intake.
  • Health Monitoring: Vital sign checks, monitoring chronic conditions, and general health assessments.

Why Choose In-Home Nursing Care?

  • Comfort and convenience: Receive professional healthcare in the comfort of your home.
  • Personalized care: Care plans are tailored to your specific needs and health conditions.
  • Independence: Continue living independently with the support of skilled professionals.
  • Proactive healthcare: Regular check-ups and management help prevent complications and hospitalizations.

Skilled Care and Safety

Our nurses are registered professionals who provide medical tasks with the highest level of care and hygiene. By handling more complex health needs, we reduce the risk of complications and provide peace of mind for both you and your family.

Support for Families and Carers

  • Reducing caregiving stress: Family carers can focus on emotional support while our nurses handle the medical tasks.
  • Training and empowerment: Nurses can also teach simple healthcare tasks to family members, empowering them to assist with care between visits.

When Do You Need Nursing Care?

If you have medical needs that require professional healthcare, community nursing can support you. This includes:

  • Chronic conditions (e.g., diabetes, heart disease)
  • Post-surgical care
  • Complex medication needs
  • Conditions like cerebral palsy, multiple sclerosis, or brain injury
