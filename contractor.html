Policy Acknowledgements (Sign Each)

By signing each section below, the Worker confirms they have read, understood, and will comply with the policy.

NDIS Code of Conduct

  • Respect rights and preferences; support self-determination.
  • Respect privacy; keep information confidential.
  • Provide safe, competent supports within scope; act with integrity.
  • Raise/act on concerns; prevent and respond to abuse/neglect.
  • No higher prices for NDIS participants for the same goods/services.

Incident Reporting & Complaints

  • Report incidents/injuries/near-misses immediately and document.
  • Escalate reportable incidents without delay; call emergency services first if needed.
  • Support complaints without reprisal; cooperate with investigations; keep confidentiality.

Privacy & Confidentiality

  • Follow Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and HD HeeD Privacy Policy.
  • Share information only with consent or legal authority; secure records; report breaches.
  • Return all records on exit; confidentiality continues after engagement.

Safeguarding & Duty of Care

  • Identify risks and take reasonable steps to prevent harm; respect dignity of risk.
  • Maintain professional boundaries; never abuse or exploit.
  • Report suspected abuse/neglect immediately; work within training/competency.

Workplace Health & Safety (WHS)

  • Take reasonable care of own and others’ safety; be fit for work.
  • Follow procedures/training; use PPE correctly.
  • Report hazards, incidents, injuries, near-misses promptly.
