Which one should I use?

Use AILR if you’re a Support Coordinator / plan manager and you want to give the whole story in one go.

Use Online referral if you’re the participant, a family member, or you’re just exploring supports.

Use Paper referral if your service requires a printable / emailable copy (hospital, GP, RN, Aged Care).
