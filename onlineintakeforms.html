Which form should I pick?

Use AILR if you’re a Support Coordinator, plan manager or caregiver with a full NDIS view and you want us to see everything at once.

Use Online Referral if you’re a participant, family, or you’re just exploring supports and have only the basics.

Use Paper / Email Referral if you must attach it to a clinical file, print for signature, or send via secure email.
