Our Services

At HD Heed, we deliver services with heart. Our dedicated team ensures your independence and wellbeing are at the centre of all support we provide.

Personal Care

Our Personal Care services assist with daily personal activities, including personal hygiene (showering, bathing, oral care), dressing, grooming, toileting, and mobility support. We aim to help participants maintain independence and dignity in their daily routines.

Community Participation

We support participants in engaging with their community through social, recreational, and educational activities. This includes assistance in attending events, joining clubs, or participating in community programs, fostering inclusion and personal growth.

Support Coordination

Our Support Coordinators help participants understand and implement their NDIS plans. We connect you with service providers, assist in managing your supports, and build your capacity to navigate the NDIS system effectively.

Respite Services

Our Respite Services offer short-term care solutions, providing participants with a safe and supportive environment while giving primary caregivers a well-deserved break. Options include in-home respite and short-term accommodation.

Nursing Care

Our qualified nurses provide medical care tailored to individual needs, including medication management, wound care, chronic disease management, and health assessments, ensuring participants receive comprehensive health support.
