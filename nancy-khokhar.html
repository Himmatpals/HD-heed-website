About Jazz

Nancy Khokhar, Registered Nurse, brings extensive frontline experience in both disability and aged-care settings. Having witnessed the profound impact of compassionate, person-centred support, Nancy is driven by a steadfast belief that every individual—regardless of age or ability—deserves to live with dignity, independence, and meaningful social connection. Nancy believes HD Heed is built on a foundation of partnership and respect, treating each participant as part of the extended family. She excels at forging genuine relationships, combining clinical rigour with warmth to co-design care plans that reflect each person’s goals, strengths, and everyday routines. From facilitating community engagement and skill-building activities to implementing tailored behaviour support strategies, Nancy ensures interventions are both safe and empowering. At HD Heed, Nancy champions a holistic vision of support—seeing the person behind the needs. Through clear, ongoing communication and collaborative review processes, she keeps participants, families, and multidisciplinary teams aligned every step of the way. Nancy’s unwavering commitment to delivering timely, high-quality care empowers participants to thrive, fostering confidence, choice, and fulfilment in their NDIS journey.
