Nancy Khokar

As a Registered Nurse with years of frontline experience in both disability and aged care, I have witnessed firsthand the difference that compassionate, consistent, and truly person-centred support can make. My journey as a healthcare professional has been driven by a deep belief that everyone—regardless of age or ability—deserves to live with dignity, independence, and connection.

Together with my fellow founders, we created HD Heed with a simple but powerful goal: to build a service that treats every participant like family, and every care plan like a partnership. I am passionate about supporting people not just with care, but with opportunities to grow, socialise, and feel empowered in their everyday lives.

At HD Heed, we see the individual behind the care needs. That’s the heart of what we do—and it’s a vision I’m proud to bring to life every day.
