Our Services

Personal Care

Assistance with everyday tasks like bathing, dressing, and grooming.

Domestic Assistance

Support with household chores including cleaning, laundry, and meal preparation to keep your home safe and welcoming.

  • Home maintenance made easy: Our support workers can assist with vacuuming, mopping, dishes, laundry, ironing, changing bed linens, taking out rubbish, and wiping surfaces.
  • Meal preparation: Help with grocery shopping, meal planning, safe use of kitchen appliances, cooking, and cleaning up afterward.
  • Light gardening: Tasks like watering plants or sweeping outdoor areas to keep surroundings safe.
  • Tailored support: Choose whether you'd like tasks done for you or with you, building independence and skills where possible.
  • Health and safety focus: A clean home prevents illness and falls while improving comfort and reducing stress for you and your carers.
  • Examples of support:
    • Vacuuming, dusting, mopping, and decluttering rooms
    • Washing, drying, folding, and ironing clothes
    • Meal assistance, including recipe guidance and safe cooking support
    • Organizing storage areas and setting routines
    • Changing lightbulbs, putting out bins, or caring for indoor plants
  • Your preferences come first: You direct what needs doing and how, ensuring your comfort and satisfaction.

Community Participation

Supporting you to engage in community activities, outings, and personal interests.

Nursing Care

Medication management, wound care, and chronic condition monitoring at home.

Daily Living Skills Support

Skill-building in cooking, budgeting, and other life tasks to support independence.

In-Home Carer Relief

Short-term support to relieve primary carers while ensuring continued care.
