Domestic Assistance

Helping you maintain a clean, safe, and comfortable home.

What We Offer

  • Complete household support: Cleaning, tidying, laundry, dishes, ironing, changing bed linens, taking out the rubbish – everything you need to maintain a clean and comfortable home.
  • Meal preparation: Assistance with grocery shopping, planning and cooking meals, using kitchen appliances safely, and cleaning up afterward.
  • Light gardening: Support with watering plants, sweeping paths, and clearing clutter from outdoor areas for safety and presentation.
  • Tailored independence: Choose to have your support worker do tasks for you, or work alongside them to learn and grow your daily living skills.
  • Supportive involvement: You can participate in tasks like sorting laundry or wiping benches while your worker handles more strenuous jobs like vacuuming.
  • Safe and healthy home: Prevent slips, reduce infection risks, and improve comfort and mental wellbeing through a hygienic environment.
  • Peace of mind for families: Families and carers get relief from constant housework, knowing your home is well looked after by trusted staff.
  • Examples of support:
    • Vacuuming, dusting, mopping, and cleaning bathrooms and kitchens
    • Washing and folding laundry, ironing, and changing linens
    • Meal planning, grocery shopping, cooking, and dishwashing
    • Reorganising rooms, labelling containers, creating cleaning schedules
    • Taking out garbage, changing lightbulbs, minor home fixes, watering plants
  • Your preferences come first: You choose what tasks are done, how, and when – so your home feels just right for you.
