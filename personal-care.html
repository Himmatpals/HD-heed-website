What We Offer

Our Personal Care services are designed to support you with respect and sensitivity while helping you remain as independent as possible.

  • Showering and bathing support – We assist with hygiene while promoting your independence and privacy.
  • Dressing and grooming – Help with clothing selection, dressing, shaving, hair care, oral hygiene and more.
  • Toileting and continence support – Including use of continence aids and assistance with safe transfers.
  • Mobility assistance – Helping you move safely around your home, including bed transfers, wheelchair use, or walking aid support.
  • Mealtime assistance – Support with eating and drinking, where needed, with dignity and comfort.
  • Tailored care plans – We adapt every service to suit your personal needs, culture, and preferences.
  • Consistent and familiar carers – We aim to provide you with known and trusted staff for continuity of care.
