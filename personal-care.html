What We Offer

Our Personal Care services are designed to support you in managing your day-to-day activities while preserving your dignity, comfort, and independence.

  • Personal Hygiene: Assistance with bathing, showering, grooming, oral hygiene, hair care, and nail care (non-clinical).
  • Dressing & Undressing: Support in choosing appropriate clothes, putting them on and taking them off with respect and dignity.
  • Toileting Assistance: Support with continence aids, using the toilet, and maintaining hygiene and comfort.
  • Mobility Support: Help with moving around the house, using walking aids, transferring from bed to chair, etc.
  • Meal Preparation: Assistance with making simple meals that suit your dietary preferences and needs.
  • Medication Prompting: Reminders to take prescribed medications (non-clinical, unless done by a nurse).
  • Skin Care: Support with applying moisturiser, monitoring for skin changes, and general care (non-wound care).
  • Morning and Evening Routines: Assistance with getting ready for the day or bedtime, including help with routines.
  • Empowerment & Independence: Our support workers are trained to encourage participation and independence wherever possible.

At HD Heed, we understand that every person is unique. Our approach is flexible and respectful, tailored to your preferences, cultural background, and daily lifestyle. Whether you need light support or more frequent personal care, our friendly and reliable staff are here to help.
