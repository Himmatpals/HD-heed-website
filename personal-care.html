Personal Care

Support that puts your dignity and independence first.

What We Offer

  • Personal Hygiene: Assistance with showering, bathing, grooming, and maintaining oral hygiene to help you feel fresh and confident every day.
  • Support with Dressing: Help with choosing appropriate clothing and getting dressed or undressed comfortably and respectfully.
  • Toileting & Continence: Assistance using the toilet, incontinence management, and maintaining hygiene and dignity.
  • Mobility Assistance: Help with walking, transferring between bed and chairs, or using mobility aids to move safely at home.
  • Meal Preparation: Light assistance in preparing and serving nutritious meals tailored to your dietary requirements.
  • Medication Reminders: Timely non-clinical reminders to take prescribed medications as directed by your GP or healthcare provider.
  • Morning and Evening Routines: Support to start or end your day comfortably with routines tailored to your needs.
  • Encouragement of Independence: Wherever possible, our trained staff will encourage and support you to do things yourself at your own pace.
  • Skin Integrity Support: Assistance applying non-clinical skincare (e.g. moisturiser) and keeping an eye on changes in skin condition.

At HD Heed, we recognise that personal care is not just about physical tasks—it’s about trust, respect, and empowering you to live life on your terms. Every person is different, and we tailor our care to suit your preferences, cultural needs, and level of comfort.
